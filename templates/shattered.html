How to Solve Shattered Veil

  1. Step 1: Look for a chalkboard near Pack-a-Punch. Grab the first set of letters and match it to your chalkboard above.
  2. Step 2: Go to Round 10, kill the special zombie, and grab the keycard.
  3. Step 3: Head to Stamin-Up and place the keycard into the printer. Grab the word that prints out.
  4. Step 4: Select the chalkboard letters and the word here — your code will appear.
