Terminus Solver

Input your X, Y, and Z values below to get the solved results.

How to Find Numbers

1. X: Find the hour hand on the clock in the Quick Revive room.
2. Y: Find the playing card on the Mess Hall bulletin board (Ace = 1).
3. Z: Go to Engineering (F5) and check the “Days Since Last Injury” sign.

Once you have X, Y, Z, enter them above and hit Solve.
